About Me

I am Mohammed Auof, a Cloud Security enthusiast based in India, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science. My primary focus is on understanding how secure access, identity, and permissions are designed and managed in modern computing environments.

I am building strong foundations in Identity and Access Management concepts, access control models, and security governance, while also strengthening my understanding of operating systems, networking, and Linux. I am particularly interested in how security principles such as least privilege and segregation of duties are applied in real world systems.

I believe in learning through hands on practice and structured exploration. Whether it is configuring access policies, studying system behavior, or strengthening core technical fundamentals, my goal is to develop practical, security focused skills with long term relevance.
